The Social Network


By Daniel Clark Creative, Fair use, Link

The Social Network is one of my all time favourite movies which never gets bored even after infinite times of rewatching. This film is directed by renowned and my personal favourite director David Fincher. This film stars Jesse Eisenberg, Andrew Garfield and Justin Timberlake in main roles. It's a semi-fictional adaptation of how the great social media platform Facebook is found. I personally like Andrew Garfield as an actor which is one of the reasons why I watched this film. At first it felt like a normal film. But after a re-watch the film just became a masterpiece to me.
